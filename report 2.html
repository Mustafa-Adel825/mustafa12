Benefits of the Internet of Things

The Internet of Things enables humans to effectively and easily control things, near and far. For example, the user can start his car's engine and control it
from his computer. One can also control the washing duties with his washing machine, and one can learn about the contents of the refrigerator remotely through the use of internet connection. However, these are examples of the
