What is required?

When talking about what is required to deal with the "internet of things" phenomenon, the list goes on. In the hardware and software production sector, market forces can ensure high quality of Internet-related things, interconnection methods, and interoperability according to global best practices. There are leading global companies in this field, investing billions of dollars in scientific research to expand the technology of understanding “things”. What is required of the governments of developed and developing countries alike, is to expand the strengthening of the infrastructure and superstructure of the Internet, especially with regard to the Internet protocol resource in the country.
